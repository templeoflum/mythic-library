The Jataka is a massive collection of Buddhist folklore about
 previous incarnations of the Buddha, both in human and animal form.
 Originally written in Pali, and dating to at least 380 BCE,
 the Jataka includes many stories which have traveled afar.
 Many of these can be traced cross-culturally in the folklore of many countries.
