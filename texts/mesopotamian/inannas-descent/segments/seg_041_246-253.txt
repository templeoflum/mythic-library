"They will
offer you a riverful of water -- don't accept it. They will offer you a field
with its grain -- don't accept it. But say to her: "Give us the corpse hanging
on the hook." (She will answer:) "That is the corpse of your queen." Say to
her: "Whether it is that of our king, whether it is that of our queen, give it
to us." She will give you the corpse hanging on the hook. One of you sprinkle
on it the life-giving plant and the other the life-giving water. Thus let
Inana
arise."
